Welcome to Natural Care Journey

Natural Care Journey is a website that offers skin products to improve the health of your skin. Whether you are a boy or girl, young or old, with oily, dry, or sensitive skin, we have researched products for you to try at various price points. NCJ also includes information about different foods that can impact how your skin behaves.

Each type of product includes an option for oily, dry, and combination skin types. We also include products at various price points with the lowest price at the top of each list. Keep in mind that you don't have to spend a lot of money to have a good skin care routine.

About Us

Natural Care Journey is a website created by Nehemie Yacinthe, Carolina Herrero, and Jenny Chen during the Girls Who Code Summer Immersion Program in Miami. Our goal with this website is to help people improve the health of their skin without having to resort to chemicals or drugs to do so. We aim to not only improve your skin health but your self-confidence as well!

Nehemie Yacinthe

Nehemie Yacinthe is a student at West Boca High School, Class of 2020. Born and raised in Boca Raton, Florida, Nehemie began struggling with her skin at 16 and didn't know where to start to improve her skin. She hopes that with this website other people like her will have a better start on how to take care of their skin and boost their self-confidence because when you look good you feel good.
