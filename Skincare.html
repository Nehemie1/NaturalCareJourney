What Cleansers Do: Cleansers remove dead skin cells, oil, and dirt from your face. This aids in unclogging pores and preventing skin conditions such as acne.

What Toners Do: Toners further removes excess dirt and oil. In addition to this, toners correct and balance the pH of your skin which helps to control acne.

What Face Oils Do: Face oils are good skin protectants. They penetrate deep into the skin, keeping water and good moisters in and keeping toxins and bad stuff out.

What Face Serums Do: Face serums are lightweight moisturizers that penetrate deeper into the skin delivering active ingredients.

What Moisturizers Do: Moisturizers help to treat your skin and prevent it from drying out.
